The Secret Realm

Journey into Kaos

Controls

  • Go - Moves your character one square.
  • Look - Take a closer look at an item.
  • Get - Pick up an item.
  • Use - Use an item.

Following the sound of water, the trees around you slowly start to dissipate, until you reach a clearing in the forest. The sun shines brightly here, bathing the open area in a warm light. Birds can be heard singing and moving in the trees around you. In front of you is a cheerful bubbling Stream. Out of the corner of your eye you notice something shining on a nearby Tree. You can exit the clearing to the West.
